    Methodology

    Figures are transcribed verbatim from the enrolled bill's General Revenue section (Title II, §1) and, for every unit shown with line items, validated in code to sum exactly to the bill's stated total. "Enacted" reflects the bill as passed; the Governor's line-item vetoes (FY2026) trim some amounts further — true post-veto figures arrive with the full build.
